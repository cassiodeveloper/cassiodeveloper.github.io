About Me.

Projects

Codes & Consequences

Board Game

Codes & Consequences is an educational and strategic game that simulates the real-world challenges of secure software development. Players take on roles within tech teams and face critical decisions between investing in features or security. The journey unfolds through development cycles inspired by agile methodologies, while players encounter real-world vulnerabilities, incidents, unexpected events, and cyber threats. The goal is to deliver a functional and secure system by balancing reputation, resources, and risk.C&C.

BSides Kraków

Cyber Security Conference

BSides events combine security expertise from a variety of platforms in search of the “next big thing” in information security. BSides is an open platform that gives security experts and industry professionals the opportunity so share ideas, insights, and develop longstanding relationships with others in the community. It is a rare opportunity to directly connect and create trusted relationships with key members of the community.BSides Kraków.

DevSecOps Podcast

Podcast

The DevSecOps Podcast is a space dedicated to the in-depth exploration of Application Security and DevSecOps, bringing objective and relevant discussions about best practices, challenges and trends in the integration of security into software development. With episodes aimed at both experienced professionals and those who are just starting out in the field, the podcast presents technical insights, interviews with experts and analyses of real cases, always focusing on the practical application of security in the development cycle.DevSecOps Podcast.

DevSecCon Poland

Meetups

We are a chapter of the Global DevSecCon Community, an inclusive community that educates and enables developers in application security. If you’re a security enthusiast & you want to learn more about how to better secure your team, then check out our community & resources.DevSecCon Poland.
